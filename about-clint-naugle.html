About Clint Naugle

Where I've Been

From a young age, I loved to draw. By high school, I was accepted into a magnet art program and began to explore the use of color through painting. My high school portfolio earned me a scholarship to Pratt Institute (in Brooklyn, NY), of which I graduated on the Dean's list in 2007. Directly out of school, I began working for JR.com, an electronics retailer in downtown NYC. There I taught myself HTML and CSS while creating email marketing that went out to over half a million subscribers. After 2 years, I was promoted to the Lead Web Designer and worked directly with upper management and the developers to overhaul the entire website. I continued to further my knowledge of coding best practices and learned a lot from the developers.

Where I Am

Currently, I am a Senior Web Designer for Newegg.com.

On a personal note, I came to the west coast in 2012 to follow my passion for surfing. I live a block from the ocean in Huntington Beach and love every minute of it.

Where I'm Going

I'm looking to expand my knowledge of UI/UX principles and front-end coding languages, as well as dip my toes into some back-end coding and database languages. I'm working towards becoming a full-stack web developer with an eye for design.
