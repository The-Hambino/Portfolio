J&R Signage

My Role: Design J&R's wayfinding signage which included over 100 interior and exterior signs.

As part of a brand refresh, J&R overhauled their signage, both interior and exterior. In New York City, J&R's shopping bag became an icon. The audaciously colored bag could be spotted from down the block or across the subway car—red logo on neon blue with white, plastic handles.

The wayfinding signage was designed to build off of the already established iconic status of the bag.
