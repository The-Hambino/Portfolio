Social Promotion for May the 4th

May the 4th / Revenge of the 5th

My Role: Design type lockup, landing pages, and banner ads for May the 4th and Revenge of the 5th.

May the 4th, also known as Star Wars Day, is very popular among the Newegg customer base and to recognise that, we put together a themed promotion tangentially referencing elements of the geek fandom. The promotion was very successful, so we extended it to May 5th, 'Revenge of the 5th.'
